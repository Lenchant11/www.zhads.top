Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started.

Is there any fee to apply?
No. We never charge application or sign-up fees. Our platform is completely free to join and use for all Canadians.
How do task compensations work?
Compensation is processed after tasks pass quality checks. Timing can vary by method and province, but most Canadians receive compensation within a reasonable time after task completion.
What equipment do I need?
A smartphone or computer with internet connection are enough for most tasks. Some roles may prefer a computer, but it's not required for the majority of our tasks available across Canada.
Is this available in all Canadian provinces?
Yes, our remote tasks are available to residents of all Canadian provinces and territories. You just need an internet connection to participate.
Is there guaranteed income?
No, task availability and compensation vary based on multiple factors including your location, skills, task completion quality, and current task availability. We provide opportunities but cannot guarantee specific income levels.
